Step 3: Welcome the visitor by having Alonzo say a message

Have Alonzo move to (190, -130) and say a welcome message whenever the green flag is clicked (at the top right). (This is the normal way Snap! projects begin; they stop by clicking the red stop sign.) Tell the user to click on Alonzo, and have the message disappear and once they do so. (This step is a little tricky; note that if Alonzo uses the Say block with a blank input, he'll stop saying things.)

You can have two "scripts" of code, each started with a "hat block" that tells the script to run. In Snap!, two scripts can run simultaneously.
