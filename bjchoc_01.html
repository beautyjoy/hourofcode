Step 2: Make Alonzo move to a random spot when clicked

Now, add blocks so that, every time Alonzo is clicked, he moves to a random position (between -190 and 190 in the x direction and between -130 and 130 in the y direction).

Blocks shaped like ovals are used to return values (they don't "do" anything). They are meant to be dragged into oval-shaped blanks, which will turn white to indicate that you can drop them in.

(Recall, to find out what a block does, you can right-click on it (control-click on a Mac) and choose "help...")
