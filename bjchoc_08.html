Step 9: Have Alonzo trace where it has been and clear traces when the player sets a new high score

Keep track of Alonzo's path using the "pen" which can be in an "up" or "down" position.

When the pen is down and Alonzo moves from one place to another, Snap! will trace Alonzo's movement.

Clear all the pen traces when the user achieves a high score.
