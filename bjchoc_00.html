Step 1: Make your first Mobile App with only 2 blocks!

Welcome to the Beauty and Joy of Computing Hour of Code. You'll be using Snap!, a graphical programming language, to make a mobile app game.

Program the yellow Alonzo sprite to turn around when clicked. Drag the two blocks from the palette (to the right of this text) to the scripting area (to the right of the palette). Blocks click together, and once together, they always run from top to bottom. To find out what a block does, right-click it (or control-click on a Mac) and choose "help..."

Once you think you're done, go to the next step by clicking the green "next" button. If you get stuck, you can either click "show me the answer" to see our version (and then fix your version), or click "replace my code" and we'll replace your solution with ours.
